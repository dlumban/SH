What are the benefits and features of ScamSafe?ScamSafe is an all-in-one app designed to protect you from scams, spam, and unwanted calls and messages,  while improving your overall mobile phone experience. Here’s what ScamSafe offers:
Auto Block Numbers: Automatically blocks suspicious calls, so your phone won’t ring for potential scams. You can still review blocked numbers in your call history.
Number Lookup and Reporting: Look up unknown numbers and report them as fraud or scams to strengthen the community database.
SMS Categorisation: Organises your SMS into folders like General, Transaction, Promotion, and Spam (Android) or Transaction, Promotion, and Junk (iOS), keeping your messages neat.
SMS Keyword Filtering: Automatically filters and sends specific SMS types (like unwanted promotions) directly to the junk folder based on your preferences.
Manual Blocking: Block specific numbers manually from call history or search results.
Whitelist Creation: Allow important numbers to bypass spam filters to ensure your don't miss those important calls.
International Call Blocking: Block international numbers to avoid unsolicited calls from outside Singapore.
Database Auto Updates: Stay protected with automatic updates to the spam number database.
New Spam Numbers Count: See how many new spam numbers have been added since your last database update.
We got you with comprehensive scam and spam protection.
